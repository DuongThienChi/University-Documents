Homework

Turn in Homework Peer Evaluation

To turn in an assignment, click the link at right to go to our "CourseWork" site, then click Dropbox from the menu at left. Find your name in the list, then click Add, then Upload Files, and follow the instructions.

Please upload a .ZIP archive containing your entire project so that it can be downloaded and run in Android Studio by the instructor or another student. Please give your file a descriptive name including your name and the assignment name; something like "JohnSmith-hw2-MrPotatoHead.zip". Please do NOT use other compression formats such as .GZ, .7Z, .RAR, etc. (If you don't know how to make a ZIP file, please see the instructions here: Windows, Mac, Linux.)
